Our Gulet

General

Features

Arabella is an Ultra-Lux, Classic Gulet, and one of the most admired yachts to sail the Mediterranean and Aegean seas. Built in 2007 it was refitted in 2017, 2018, 2019 and most recently in Spring 2020. A total of 28 meters in length with a 7.10 meter beam. It boasts two 540 BHP engines and a top speed of 12 Knots. It has two 27kw & one 11 kw generators for providing electric all round, and a 4000 liter- 150 lt/hour fresh water tank . Originally registered under the American Flag, it was re-registered under the Turkish Flag in 2019.

 

Interior

Features

Beautifully fitted with an extensive wood finish, Arabella is the true meaning of luxury. The cabins and living areas offer the space and comfort of larger yachts, all while still providing an intimate cruising experince for upto 8 people. 2 Master & VIP cabins and 2 twin single cabins, each with en-suite bathrooms. A living area that is fully equipped with a large dining table, a comfortable seating area, a large tv with satellite and air-conditioning. Also a separate, fully equipped bar and kitchen.

 

 

Our Cabins

Four Luxury Cabins

All fully air conditioned for a comfortable luxurious stay for up to 8 people.

The Master Cabin

The Master Cabin is decorated beautifully, with a fine wooden finish and carpeted for a soft touch under foot. A King-Size bed, TV with sattelite, wardrobe, make-up table, and a spacious en-suite bathroom with jacuzzi bath, separate shower and his & hers sinks.

The VIP Cabin

The VIP Cabin is also decorated with the same fine finish and carpeted floors. A Large double bed, wardrobes, TV with satellite, and an en-suite bathrom with shower & wc facilities.

The Twin Cabins

Fitted with two single beds in each, our two Twin cabins also have carpeted floors, an en-suite bathroom & shower facilities in both cabins, and also have a large satellite tv.
