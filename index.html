Our Mission

Never Stop Exploring

With so many beautiful places to visit along the coastline of Turkey, what better way to explore than on your own private gulet! Exceptional landmarks and historical legacies, wonderful natural scenery and a rich culture, Turkey is definitely the jewel of the Mediterranean. Aboard Arabella, the perfect Turkish experience is just around the corner!

Aboard Arabella, we have been bringing the luxury, quality and service of a 5 star hotel to the sea since 2007. From our Captain to our Service Staff, our whole crew are ready to serve your every need with the highest standard of professionalism.
