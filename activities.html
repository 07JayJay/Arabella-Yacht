Off-Board Activities

With so many different places to explore around Turkey, and not all of them are accessable by sea, why not let us arrange a day trip on land for you and your family? Go out and discover some of the many hidden treasures of the Turquoise Coast. Whether it be to explore Myra, in Demre, home to some of the most impressive and well preserved ruins in all of Turkey, or to visit thebeautiful beach at the Blue Lagoon in Ölüdeniz, Fethiye. Rest assured there's somewhere you haven't yet seen!

It's easy to build up a good appetite after spending the day exploring, we can also make reservations at local restaurants, bistros, bars & clubs at any of the destinations along your Blue Voyage. You can try out many of the local food dishes with a traditional meze to accompany your meal, or why not try the freshly caught fish brought in that day by local fishermen! Bodrum, full of beautiful beaches, great restaurants and an intense nightlife is a great stop off point to have a day and night on land.
